Areas I Serve

Lyons

The red-rock gateway to the Rockies.

Where the plains meet the mountains, Lyons is a charming small town wrapped in red sandstone bluffs at the confluence of the North and South St. Vrain Creeks. It's the gateway to the high country and Rocky Mountain National Park, and a beloved music destination — home to the RockyGrass and Folks festivals at Planet Bluegrass.

With a tight-knit, artistic community and a walkable historic downtown, Lyons offers everything from in-town cottages to riverside and mountain properties. It's a special place for buyers who want small-town character with the Rockies in their backyard.
