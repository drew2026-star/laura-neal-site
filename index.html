Meet Laura

An artist's eye,
a teacher's patience.

Before Laura helped people find homes, she was a teacher, an artist, and a mural painter. She came to Colorado from Texas for college at CU Boulder, spent a season with AmeriCorps in Montana, and returned to the Front Range in 2011 to put down roots.

Today she lives in Lyons with her family and pets — and brings an artist's eye and a teacher's patience to every transaction. Her goal is simple: make buying or selling your home feel exciting and empowering, never overwhelming.
