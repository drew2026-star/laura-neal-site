Areas I Serve

Boulder

Flatirons, Pearl Street, and endless trails.

Framed by the iconic Flatirons and anchored by the University of Colorado, Boulder blends outdoor adventure with a vibrant downtown, a renowned dining scene along Pearl Street, and one of the country's most dynamic tech and startup communities. Thousands of acres of protected open space and trails sit right at the city's edge.

Boulder's housing is as varied as its neighborhoods — historic homes near downtown and University Hill, modern condos and lofts, and sought-after properties tucked against the foothills. It's a competitive, high-demand market where local guidance makes a real difference.
